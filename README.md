### About

Founder at **TourScale** &mdash; building software and infrastructure for a multi-brand experiential tourism company with 80+ franchise locations across the US. I handle everything from marketing sites to internal platforms to bare-metal servers.

Brands include **Cruisin' Tikis**, **Trolley Pub**, **Paddle Pub**, **Tiki Pub**, plus **TourCraft** (manufacturing) and **ListMyFleet** (B2B marketplace).
